### Lionel Messi 🐐

<img src="/messi.webp" alt="Lionel Messi" style="width:300px; float:right; margin-left:20px;">

The greatest ever. The GOAT. I have been following him since 2009 (when I was 4 years old). Even though I did not understand much of football back then, I knew only Messi as I read in the newspaper in 2009 that FC Barcelona won the Champions League (that too against A. Ferguson's Man Utd).

Saddest moments:
- 2014 World Cup final loss
- 2015 Copa America final loss
- 2016 Copa America final loss
- 2018 elimination against France
- 2019 UCL match at Anfield
- 2020 UCL match against Bayern

The joyous moments:
- 2015 Champions League win
- 2021 Copa America win against Neymar's Brazil
- 2022 Finallisma win against Euro champions Italy
- 2022 FIFA World Cup win against Mbappe's France
